VEG SALAD




INGREDIENTS


1 Cup of Avocado
1 cup of Cucumbers, Cherry Tomatoes or Bell Peppers
1 cup of Green Beans
1 cup of Roasted Beets
1 cup of corn Kernels
1 cup of Broccoli
1/2 cup of yogurt (add Spieces if required)
1/2 cup of cool whip (or whip your own cream)



  STEPS TO MAKE VEG SALAD


  •     Place all of the prepared Vegetables in a medium bowl

  •     Stir in Yogurt and Cool Whip.

  •     Refrigerate before serving.

  •     Sample the deliciousness… then gobble up a little more


BENEFITS OF VEG SALAD


vegetables are a great source of vitamins and minerals. You won’t find a better nutritional source than fruits and vegetables, which are packed with vitamins A, C and E, as well as magnesium, zinc, phosphorous and folic acid. For potassium, one of the most important minerals for your health, eat plenty of avocados, sweet potatoes, bananas, prunes and even tomato paste puree.
